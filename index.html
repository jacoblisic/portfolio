    About Me

    My name is Jacob Lisic. I design and develop websites and digital content in the Cleveland/Akron, Ohio area.

    I am a senior at Kent State University pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Digital Sciences and a minor in User Experience Design. I will be graduating in May 2019.

    I am experienced in HTML, CSS, SASS, and Responsive Web Design. I also have experience with JavaScript, WordPress, Adobe Creative Cloud, Webflow, and Craft CMS.

    This past summer, I had the amazing opportunity to work as a Web Developer/UX Intern at The Impact Group in Hudson, Ohio. I was involved with multiple web projects that helped me learn more about simplicity in design.

    I recently started a new position as a Digital Project Manager at IdeaBase in Kent, Ohio. IdeaBase is a student-run design agency affiliated with Kent State. I'll be adding projects I've worked on as I continue my new position so check back later if you want to learn more.

    Lastly, if you’d like to view my résumé, please do so. Or, visit my LinkedIn profile to learn more about me!
