    jacoblisic.com

    I created this website during December 2017 through February 2018. My intension was to create a portfolio website that had a very clean design. This would then help me develop a website that had a great user experience and something that people will want to interact with.

    I also wanted the website to have subtle animations that created a unique environment. I did this through buttons, links, and JavaScript animations throughout the site.

    My development process included:

    • Researched and compared the portfolio websites of multiple people over the web.
    • Created and selected the content to display on the website, including creating the logo and the text you are reading right now.
    • Sketched numerous ideas of what the pages may look like.
    • Developed the final website to publish.
    • Tested the website on multiple browsers, making sure it was compatible with each of them.
    • Launched the portfolio website.

    Buckeye Bed and Breakfast

    During the fall 2017 semester, I was tasked with creating the design and prototypes for a mock bed and breakfast called, Buckeye Bed and Breakfast.

    Specifically, I did the following:

    • Researched competing bed and breakfasts, listing frequent content on all websites that would help me decide what content to use for Buckeye Bed and Breakfast.
    • Gathered content provided to me and choose which content needed to be implemented into the website.
    • Sketched mockup pages of what I wanted the design of the website to look like.
    • Created a style tiles for the website, along with a working interactive prototype.
    • Developed final website templates based off of my research, sketched, and style tiles.
    • Executed usability and technical testing.
    • Created a pattern library based off of the final objects of the website.
