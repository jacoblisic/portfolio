    jacoblisic.com

    I created this website during December 2017 through January 2018. My intension was to create a portfolio website that had a very clean design, making the user experience the best it could be. I wanted the website to have subtle animations that created a unique environment.

    My development process included:

    • Researching and comparing multiple different portfolio websites on the web.
    • Created the content for my website.
    • Sketched what the pages may look like after development.
    • Developed the final website.
    • Tested the website on multiple browsers.
    • Launched the portfolio website.

    Buckeye Bed and Breakfast

    I was recently enrolled in a responsive web design course. During the semester, I was tasked with creating the design and prototypes for a mock bed and breakfast called, Buckeye Bed and Breakfast.

    Specifically, I did the following:

    • Researched competing bed and breakfasts, listing frequent content on all websites that would help me decide what content to use for Buckeye Bed and Breakfast.
    • Gathered content provided to me and choose which content needed to be implemented into the website.
    • Sketched mockup pages of what I wanted the design of the website to look like.
    • Created a style tiles for the website, along with a working interactive prototype.
    • Developed final website templates based off of my research, sketched, and style tiles.
    • Executed usability and technical testing.
    • Created a pattern library based off of the final objects of the website.
